Some Stuff About Me

Who

Hello, my name is Ian Edwards and I am a london based digital designer with experience in both agency and in house roles as well as some experience working freelance (both good and bad).

Web design I would have to say is my real passion in life and I have had the oportunity to work with some amazing people. I love working in an industry where there is always something new to learn.

My other pasions include working with arduino and raspberry pi, physics and Circus skills.

What

I have had experience with illutration, flash, photoshop and coding. I am able to see a project through to the end from brainstorming, user experience, graphic design and front-end development (although I would never claim to be a front-end developer).

I have a keen interest in all aspects of design but I have a real passion for code, especially how it has moved on so much in recent years. I love learning about new technologies and my main ambition is to learn enough to eventually become the internet.

Where

My work experience so far includes working for SAY Media, as a junior designer where I worked on mocking up ad campaigns.

LBI where I worked on two high profile projects one for a pitch for kurt Geigers "everything but the shoes" campaign and the website for Turkcell.

Media Ingenuity, working on their financial comparisson site TotallyMoney.com as well as ad banners, emails and websites for some of their other brands and I also worked for a breif stint as a Sandwich Artist in Subway (i'm not kidding that was my actual job title)
